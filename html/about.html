Description

This project is a small website that presents novels and their details written by Stephen King and present history of the author.

Motivation

Presented website is close to the interests of mine. I am passionate about the Stephen's King literature. I decided to create a fandom wiki for people that share this passion with me.

Vision

Application presents books written by Stephen King and more about plots. Website also fetches films from IMDB created base on Stephen's King novels also with other information about cast.
